## Full Description

Epanalipsi is a period tracking app built with one principle: your health data belongs to you.

Unlike other cycle trackers, Epanalipsi stores everything locally on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync. No analytics. No data collection of any kind. Your most personal health information never leaves your phone.

FEATURES

• Track period start dates and cycle lengths
• View cycle history with averages, shortest, and longest cycles
• Get next-period predictions based on your personal patterns
• Log symptoms (cramps, headache, fatigue, mood changes, and more) with intensity levels
• Filter and review symptom history to identify patterns
• Set reminders for upcoming periods
• Optional Garmin watch integration for heart rate and stress data
• Export your data in open formats — no lock-in
• Dark theme support

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

• 100% offline — works without internet
• No account or registration required
• No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
• No advertising
• Data stored in a local database protected by Android's app sandbox
• Cloud backup disabled by default
• Open source — verify our privacy claims yourself
• GDPR and CCPA compliant by design

WHY EPANALIPSI?

"Epanalipsi" means "remembrance" in Greek. We built this app because we believe period tracking should be private, simple, and respectful. After high-profile data breaches in health apps, we wanted to prove that a cycle tracker doesn't need your data to be useful.

Epanalipsi requires only one optional permission — notifications for period reminders — and works perfectly without it.

Your body, your data, your device. Nothing more.
